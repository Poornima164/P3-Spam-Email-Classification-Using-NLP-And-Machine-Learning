Spam Examples:

1. Lose 10 kg in just 10 days with this magical pill! Order now! 
2. Upgrade your internet speed to 1Gbps for just $1/month! Limited time offer. Click here!  
3. Get rich quick! Invest $100 today and earn $10,000 by next week. Guaranteed results!  
4. Your account has been flagged for suspicious activity. Verify your information immediately to avoid closure.  
5. Hurry! Exclusive deal just for you: Buy one smartphone, get one free! Offer ends in 24 hours!  

Not Spam Examples:  

1. We are updating our privacy policy to better serve you. Please review the changes here.  
2. Congratulations on reaching your 5th anniversary with us! We’re thrilled to celebrate your milestone.  
3. Don’t forget: Your quarterly meeting with the project team is scheduled for tomorrow at 3 PM.  
4. We’ve noticed you haven’t logged in for a while. Explore new features we’ve recently launched!  
5. Thank you for your feedback on our latest product. Your suggestions help us improve!
